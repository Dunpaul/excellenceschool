A word from our principal director: Moses Wokono

THE EXCELLENCE SCHOOL is a medium private school in the central part slums of kawangware and another new branch in the core slums of GATINA. We are the only school around or may be in the midst of very few others that grapples with the fundamental issues of not only the students/pupils and teachers but extends the same to the parents.

Over the years, our able teachers have and still demonstrate a strong dextral ability (work with dexterity) and understanding in and out of class. Our teachers are not only precise in their concept delivery but are also able to hold complex and stretched discussion with our youthful student on the emerging issues passé.

As a way of complying with the new curriculum dispensation, both our schools participated in Term one sports activities. It was both in football both boys and girls and Athletics.

For instance our boys and did extremely well. Most of them propelled to the REGIONAL LEVEL. To break further the good news, one of our Girls EASTERBEL MIRABEL MISANGA is among the many athletes who will be representing NAIROBI REGION in athletics in Mombasa.

As we thrive and strive to SHOW THE WAY the CREAM OF THE CROP in this region of Kawangware, we don’t underestimate being bogged down in simultaneous attempted tragic effort by trying and upcoming competitors and EDUCATION DUPLICATORS or rivals. We make informed efforts to LIVE in DIALOGUE rather than MONOLOGUE to build a strong shoulder within and without. THE EXCELLENCE SCHOOL MUST PRODUCE HEROES..!!

In this era of business liberties and duplication of projects, we desire to stay relevant to the course and cause of call in the community; OFFERING QUALITY AND AFFORDABLE EDUCATION TO ALL children in this slum and its environs!

As if that is not enough, despite our competitors looking at us with BIG EYES OF JINGOISM, We Thrive and Strive! We shall neither live in fear to do what we can nor fear to live to do what we anticipate and schedule to do the next thing as THE EXCELLENCE SCHOOL.

We shall Live to and Endeavour by all means to avoid artificial stratifications and social caging and usury by virtue of; who are the owners? Where do they hail from? Forward we shall move on, for God has granted us the ability.

I wish you all the very best in all your busy schedules, may you have the protection and blessings of God in all your particular undertakings

Conclusion

I allude to what Martin Luther Said,

“In the end we will not only remember the oppression from the bad people but the appalling silence of the good people during the crisis”

One time, the trumpet of hope shall blow and we will be rejoicing because we never gave up.

It’s also said that if you see something that is not right, not just you have a moral obligation to do something about. To all parents, partners, teachers and well-wishers as a whole, you are doing just that to the children in kawangware through THE EXCELLENCE SCHOOL.

The Excellence School is a distinguished educational institution that caters to students across various stages of their academic journey, from kindergarten through primary, junior secondary, and senior secondary levels.
