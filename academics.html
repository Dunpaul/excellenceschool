The Excellence School Academics

The Excellence School is a distinguished educational institution that caters to students across various stages of their academic journey, from kindergarten through primary, junior secondary, and senior secondary levels. Here are some key features of the school:

  1. Comprehensive Education: The school offers a comprehensive educational program that covers a wide range of subjects and disciplines, ensuring that students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for future success.
  2. Kindergarten: The kindergarten program provides a nurturing and supportive environment for young learners, focusing on early childhood development and foundational skills such as language, numeracy, and socialization.
  3. Primary School: In the primary school division, students continue to build upon their foundational knowledge and skills, engaging in a curriculum that emphasizes literacy, numeracy, science, social studies, and other core subjects.
  4. Junior Secondary School: As students progress to junior secondary school, they delve deeper into subject areas and begin to explore more specialized topics. The curriculum is designed to promote critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity, while also preparing students for the challenges of senior secondary education.
  5. Senior Secondary School In the senior secondary school division, students undertake a rigorous academic program that prepares them for higher education or entry into the workforce. The curriculum is aligned with the new competency-based curriculum in Kenya, emphasizing practical skills, real-world applications, and holistic development.
  6. High-Quality Education: The Excellence School is committed to providing high-quality education at all levels, employing qualified teachers, utilizing modern teaching methods and resources, and fostering a culture of academic excellence and innovation.
  7. Compliance with Competency-Based Curriculum: The school is fully compliant with the new competency-based curriculum introduced in Kenya. This curriculum focuses on developing competencies such as communication, critical thinking, collaboration, creativity, and character development, preparing students for success in the 21st century.

Overall, The Excellence School offers a dynamic and enriching educational experience that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and character traits needed to thrive in an increasingly complex and interconnected world. By embracing the new competency-based curriculum and providing high-quality education across all levels, the school empowers students to reach their full potential and become responsible global citizens.
